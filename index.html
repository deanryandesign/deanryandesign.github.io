NUBIVAGANT

  • NUBIVAGANT
    • This is a poster from a 2 hour workshop that we did. We were given a word that is no longer used in the dictionary and had to create a poster based around this word. We worked in pairs for this workshop. We were given the word Nubivagant which means through the clouds. We based our concept around headspace and created a light poster that was printed on a light stock paper that would flutter when passed with movement.

ISTD

  • ISTD
    • This was my entry to the International Society of Typographic Designers. I created this book as a guideline for people that wanted to find out more about addiction and what they could be addicted to. The book is intended to develop a connection with the reader so they can also question themselves while reading the book. My visual approach to the book is informed by progression, control, chaos and repetition and the other symptoms of addiction. I wanted to give some information about some addictions that started back throughout the years. The book reads in a way that keeps the reader questioning themselves throughout.

VISUAL FICTION

  • VISUAL FICTION
    • This project was a collaboration with a famous Irish writer Nuala O' Connor. I was given a short story and had to visualise and recreate my own interpretation of it. I based my concept and visuals around key points of the story. I created a book to gave the reader an experience of the narrative.
